Available roles at our Bugle site

  • Trainee Butcher

    As a Trainee Butcher , you’ll be learning one of the oldest crafts in the world. Our trainee programme provides an exceptional level of training and support, to give you all the skills, knowledge and behaviours needed for your rewarding career in butchery.

    £12-15 per hour + production bonus

  • Production Operatives

    Working in the onward processing of pork produce, you could be working in curing, cooking and smoking. Along with your pay, there is ample opportunity to increase your earning with the production bonus scheme, rewarding you for what you bring to the table.

    £9.76 per hour + production bonus
